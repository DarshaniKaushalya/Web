Buddhist Society

Buddhist Society is a main society from all the societies & clubs which are in the NSBM Green University. All the events related to the buddhism is organized by the Buddhist Society. There are monthly and annually organized programs in the university.


  • Annual pirith chanting ceremony
  • Donation programs
  • Programs on Vesak Poya Day (Vesak Kalapa)
  • Programs on Poson Poya Day (Poson Kalapa)
  • Bhakthi Geetha programs
  • Dhamma sermons & sethpirith programs for new students
  • Medical checkup programs
